Onboarding Guide

To get the most out of the TREA365 platform, you will need to enter as much information about your company and its operations as possible. This guide will help you get started loading some of that information into the system.

Because some types of information are dependent on others, you will find that you are unable to access certain sections (the buttons are disabled) until the appropriate pre-requisites exist.
